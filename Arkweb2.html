More About CivilizationRp ARK

RULES

-Stay in your role-play character while in the server.

-Before roleplaying join our discord and xbox club. To find it and join it it will show on the top of your screen when you join.

-No offline raiding.

-If you die you can keep your character if a admin lets you.

-You can betray your faction or group.

-You cant join more then 1 faction or group.

-And of course no bullying.

How to join a group or a faction?

Easy, go in our discord server and go at the channel called faction-recruitment.

GROUP

How to make a group to a faction.

You need 5-10 players in your tribe+you need your own base=group


FACTION

You need 10+ players in your tribe+Your own base+A farm+You need citizens=Faction 

Militia

You need 5+ players in your tribe+Your own base+Armory+Rankings for your soldiers+outfits for your soldiers+weapons=Militia

How to sign up your group, faction or militia?

Just ask an admin
